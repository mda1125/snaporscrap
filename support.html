How to Use Snap or Scrap

How do I start cleaning my library?

  1. Open Snap or Scrap
  2. Grant photo library access when prompted
  3. Wait for the initial scan to complete
  4. Tap any category card (Screenshots, Blurry Photos, etc.)
  5. Start swiping! Left to delete, right to keep

How long does the initial scan take?

The first scan depends on your library size:

  • • 1,000 photos: ~30 seconds
  • • 5,000 photos: ~2 minutes
  • • 10,000+ photos: ~5 minutes

The app must remain open during the initial scan. After that, rescans are faster.

How do I delete photos?

  1. Choose a category to clean up
  2. Swipe left on photos you want to delete
  3. Swipe right on photos you want to keep
  4. After reviewing up to 50 photos, tap "Review Deletions"
  5. Confirm the deletion queue
  6. Tap "Delete All" to permanently remove photos

Can I undo a deletion?

Before confirming deletion, you can restore any photo from the review screen. Once you tap "Delete All" and confirm, photos are permanently removed from your library.

Note: Deletions go to the "Recently Deleted" album where you have 30 days to recover them.

help FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

help_outline Will this app delete photos I want to keep?

No! You review every photo before deletion. You can also restore photos from the review screen before confirming.

help_outline Is this safe to use?

Yes. The app uses iOS standard deletion methods. Deleted photos go to "Recently Deleted" where you have 30 days to recover them.

help_outline Does the app upload my photos anywhere?

No. All analysis happens on your device. Your photos never leave your iPhone. See our Privacy Policy for details.

help_outline Can I use the app offline?

Yes! The app works completely offline. No internet connection required.

help_outline What does "Hidden Gems" mean?

Hidden Gems are high-quality photos you may have forgotten about. Our AI analyzes face detection, image composition, photo sharpness, and how long ago the photo was taken. Older, high-quality photos with clear subjects rank higher.

help_outline How is this different from the iOS Photos app?

iOS Photos doesn't automatically categorize screenshots, blurry photos, or duplicates. Snap or Scrap uses AI to identify cleanup opportunities and makes deletion fast with swipe gestures.

help_outline Will this delete my iCloud photos too?

If you use iCloud Photos, deletions sync across all devices. Photos moved to "Recently Deleted" will be removed from iCloud after 30 days.

help_outline What's the difference between duplicates and similar photos?

Duplicates: Exactly the same photo saved twice. Similar Photos: Photos taken seconds apart (like burst mode) that look nearly identical but aren't exact copies.

build Troubleshooting

Common Issues

The app says "No photos found" in a category

This means the scan didn't find any photos matching that category. For example:

  • • No screenshots = no screenshots in your library
  • • No blurry photos = all your photos are sharp!

Photos aren't deleting

Make sure you:

  1. Swipe photos left to mark them
  2. Tap "Review Deletions"
  3. Confirm each photo
  4. Tap "Delete All" and confirm

If photos still aren't deleting, check if iOS is preventing deletion (read-only albums or synced photos can't be deleted).

The app is slow or freezing

Try these steps:

  1. Close and reopen the app
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Check for iOS updates
  4. Make sure you have at least 1GB free storage

If issues persist, contact support.

Statistics aren't updating

Stats update after each session. If they're not updating:

  1. Complete a full cleanup session
  2. Confirm deletion
  3. Check the Progress dashboard

If still not working, try rescanning your library from Settings.
