Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

help Does Snap or Scrap upload my photos to the cloud?

No! Your photos never leave your device. All scanning and analysis happens locally on your iPhone. We never upload, store, or have access to your photos.

help Can I recover photos after deleting them?

Yes! Deleted photos go to a temporary trash bin first. You have 30 days to recover them before they're permanently removed. You can also review your trash at any time within the app.

help Why do I need to grant photo library access?

We need access to your photo library to display and help you organize your photos. This is the only way the app can function. Rest assured, we only use this access to show you your photos—nothing is uploaded or shared.

help How does the blur detection work?

We use on-device machine learning to analyze image sharpness and detect motion blur. The analysis happens entirely on your iPhone and works even without an internet connection.

help Is the app free to use?

Yes! Snap or Scrap is free to download and use. We may offer optional premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always be free.

help The app is running slowly. What can I do?

If you have a very large photo library (50,000+ photos), the initial scan may take a few minutes. Try closing other apps to free up memory. If issues persist, please email us with your device model and iOS version.
