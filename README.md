# Snap or Scrap

**Reclaim Your Storage. Rediscover Your Memories.**

Snap or Scrap is an iOS app that helps you quickly clean up your photo library by letting you swipe through screenshots, blurry photos, duplicates, and large videos—then delete them in seconds.

## Features

- **Swipe to Decide** – Quickly review photos with intuitive swipe gestures
- **Smart Categories** – Automatically groups screenshots, blurry photos, duplicates, and large videos
- **Bulk Delete** – Remove unwanted photos in seconds
- **Rediscover Memories** – Surface forgotten gems you'll love seeing again
- **Privacy First** – All processing happens on your device
